Creamy Cajun Shrimp Pasta with Sausage

Description

This Cajun Chicken and Sausage Pasta is chock full of smoked sausage and chicken in a creamy, cheesy cajun sauce. It's the perfect quick and easy, dinner recipe for busy weeknights!

Ingredients

  • Smoke Sausage
  • Chicken
  • Penne Pasta
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Green Bell Pepper
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Onion
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Olive Oil
  • Whipping Chream
  • Fresh Garlic
  • Cajun Seasoning
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the directions on the package and drain the water when done.
  2. Slice the sausage into 1/2 inch circles and cube the chicken.
  3. Place olive oil in a skillet. Once skillet is heated cook the sausage and chicken.
  4. Stir in a little bit of cajun seasoning and garlic powder with the meat. Empty the sausage and chicken into a bowl and set aside.
  5. Dice the bell peppers, onions, and chop the garlic. Melt one tablespoons of butter in the skillet. Add the the vegetables and saute on low heat until tender. Drain any liquid.
  6. Melt half stick of butter in the skillet and slowly add a cup of whipping cream, a little bit at a time. Stir in one tablespoon of cajun seasoning and 1/2 cup of shredded parmesan cheese. Stir until the sauce thickens.
  7. Mix in the chicken and sausage. Then toss in the pasta. Donâ€™t forget to top the pasta with the remaining 1/2 cup of parmesan cheese.
  8. Sprinkle a little bit of cajun seasoning over the pasta dish and top with chopped parsley. Serve while hot.
  9. Enjoy!
